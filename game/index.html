Growing Food at Home

DID YOU KNOW?

  • ✔ Home gardens can produce vegetables for as little as R2–R5 per kg, compared to R15–R40 at shops.
  • ✔ Fresh, home-grown produce retains more vitamins and minerals because it goes straight from soil to plate.
  • ✔ When you grow your own food, you decide what goes on it — no harmful pesticides or chemicals.
  • ✔ Community and home food gardens are a key strategy recommended by the UN to fight food insecurity.
  • ✔ South Africa has over 13 million people who are food insecure — home gardens can make a real difference.
